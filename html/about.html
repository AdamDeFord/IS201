Washington Born Family RaisedContact

The sixth and last child of Doug and Susan DeFord, Adam understands very well the blessings of family. He counts it one of life's greatest blessings to have a close and wonderful relationship with his siblings and parents.

This familial closeness feeds into Adam's religious devotion. A committed member of the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints, Adam loves to serve and uplift individuals and his community.

When he is not making journal entries or fussing with spreadsheets, Adam is likely to be found on the stage. Theatre has been his passion for many years, and he is happy to have been able to share that passion with thousands of audience members.
