Global + Superstore Data

These graphs draw information gathered from global superstores from Jan 2011 to Dec 2014.

Profits by state shows the total profit earned by each state. California earned the most with over $76k.

California 2-Year Profits dives deeper into California's earnings by showing them by date. The stores there earned their highest profits in November of 2013

California Profits by Category further breaks down California's profits into the individual categories of goods. Office Supplies was the most profitable category.

The final graph, Forecasted Office Supply Sales uses past data to forecast future sales of Office Supplies through November of 2015.
